  • EEE Leaderhsip Immersion Programme 2019 to Taipei, Taiwan

  • Summer Exchange Programme 2018 at Hanyang International Summer School in Seoul, South Korea
  • TThe Hanyang International Summer School is a 4-week academic program (2-27 July 2018) offered by Hanyang University in Seoul, Korea for students from all over the world. This program is designed to provide the opportunity for students to take up to 3 academic courses from an offering of over 120 in 10 different fields of study, while being exposed to a variety of unique cultural experiences of Korean culture.
